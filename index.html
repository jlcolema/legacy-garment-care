Legacy Garment Care is a family owned and operated business since 1992. Our company began by buying out the establishment where our founder worked. Through years of effort and dedication, we now have a number of retail locations in the metro area. We take great pride in our work and continue to make strides in providing high quality garment care to all of our clients. Our production plant consists of 10,000 square feet, where our staff has access to the most advanced equipment that results in a finish product that compares to none. Legacy is also conscious of the ecological changes in our environment. We make strides in using the most eco-friendly methods in the industry.

Legacy Garment Care stands apart from other cleaners by providing individual attention to every customer and their garments. From casual wear to evening attire, Legacy Garment Care offers a full service line including:

  • Fine dry cleaning
  • Wedding Gown Preservation
  • Shirt Laundering
  • Fine Linen service
  • Household Laundry
  • Fur / Suede / Leather service
  • Custom Tailoring
  • Apartment Cleaning Service
  • Shoe Repair
  • Winter / Summer storage
  • Package service for non-doorman buildings

Legacy Garment Care has a number of stores citywide. You can visit any of our locations or can call our toll free number to arrange for in-home pick and delivery service. We service both doorman-buildings and non-doorman buildings. We provide convenient express bags with name tags that allow for quick drop-off. We welcome individual and corporate accounts. At Legacy Garment Care, no account is too small or too large to handle.

Legacy Garment Care typically returns items within 2 business days. However, we can accommodate to provide same day service on most items, provided we received them by 9 a.m on operation days.
