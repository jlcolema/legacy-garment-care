Call us today at 1 (866) 95-Legacy

We want our clients to look and feel good! Quality is our mantra. From casual wear and business attire to white tie apparel, Legacy Garment Care has you covered.

We focus on exceptional customer service and extraordinary cleaning. Our mission is to return your garments in the best possible condition. We look for any open seams, cracked buttons and loose threads and repair them. Legacy Garment Care carries on the family tradition of expert stain removal and garment restoration. The result is a finished product that compares to no other.

To give you the best service, we serve both doorman- and non-doorman buildings. We also provide convenient custom express bags with name tags to allow for quick pick-up and drop-off.
